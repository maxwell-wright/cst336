Types of Viruses



History of the Virus Types of Viruses Do's/Don'ts Removal




  • Boot Sector Virus: Spread through removable media, this type of virus is very difficult to remove.
  • Direct Action Virus: Also called non-resident virus, these types are installed or stay hidden attached to the types of files it infects. These don't affect the user experience.
  • Resident Virus: These types of virus are difficult to identify and difficult to remove. These types can only be installed.
