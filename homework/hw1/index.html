A computer virus is a program that is designed with the purpose of invading vulnerable systems and gain access to private data. The first known computer virus was developed in 1971 by Robert Thomas, an engineer at BBN Technologies. The virus's name was the Creeper and the term computer virus wasn't coined until 1983 with Fred Cohen's paper, "Computer Viruses - Theory and Experiments".
